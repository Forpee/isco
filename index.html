CARIBBEAN FOOTBALL TRUST LTD

Our company, CARIBBEAN FOOTBALL TRUST LTD (CFTL), was given the mandate by the friends of football in the Caribbean to collaborate with other commercial partners in securing a new and more commercialized Professional Football Tournament, scheduled as an off-season tournament, based in one single marketplace in the Pan- Caribbean.

OUR CHARITY PROGRAM

To engage various members of the head of states within the Pan-Caribbean and Africa region through the efforts of the United Nations and the Rome Vatican to participate in the (“KICK POVERTY OUT ”) campaign, that will renew the deteriorated hope for most of these targeted youth and families’ vision for the future.

In our research and consideration, we have agreed on two (2) different properties as follows:

1
Meals and Nutrition Before and After School.
2
Recreation During and After School.
3
ISCO Books-to-Go Support Within the Communities.
3
Books-to-Go Support within the Communities via CFTL on going Commitment.
3
Structural Commitment.
3
Proposed LEGENDS ALL STAR GAMES in aid of hurricane victims in the Caribbean.

Our Vision

We will be embarking on establishing a new and more commercialized PanCaribbean/Latin American Professional Football event, scheduled as an off-season tournament - not a League, based in selected countries, to be called the ISLANDS CUP OPEN (ISCO) infused with internationally known Legends Marquee players and young available talent within the Pan-Caribbean and Latin American regions.
